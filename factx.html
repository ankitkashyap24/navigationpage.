FACTS ABOUT SPACE

1)The Milky Way Galaxy: Our home galaxy is a spiral-shaped disk containing billions of stars, including our Sun.

2)Black Holes: These incredibly dense objects have such strong gravity that nothing, not even light, can escape.

3)One million Earths could fit inside the Sun and the Sun is considered an average-size star.

4)The Milky Way is one of an estimated 100 billion galaxies in the universe.

5)Milky Way smells of rum, raspberries and booze
